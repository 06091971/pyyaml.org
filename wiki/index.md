## PyYAML

[PyYAML](/wiki/PyYAML) is the next generation YAML parser and emitter for
Python.  Also visit [PyYAML Documentation](/wiki/PyYAMLDocumentation).

## LibYAML

[LibYAML](/wiki/LibYAML) is a YAML parser and emitter written in C.
